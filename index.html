Before our first lesson, you’re going to build something cool: your own Explorer Profile — a short page all about you, in English.

★ TASK A

Who I Am

Copy these three lines into your notebook. Change only the colored part to make it true for you.

MODEL
Hi, I’m Ali.
I’m eleven.
I’m in 5th grade.
🎯 That’s it — just three lines!
★ TASK B

My People

Now write about your family. Write one line for each person who lives with you — and add your pet if you have one. Use his for boys, her for girls.

My Day

Last task! Write about a normal day. Start each line with a time word.

MODEL — A NORMAL DAY

Then I have breakfast and go to school.
After school, I play soccer with my friends.
